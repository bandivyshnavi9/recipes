Ingredients:
 - 1 packet maggie
 - 1 cup mixed veggies
 - 3 teaspoon oil
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 2 cups water

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, add oil.
  2. Add mixed veggies and stir until they are translucent.
  3. Add salt and water, wait until water starts bubbling.
  4. Now add maggie masala and maggie.
  5. Stir until water get evaporated.
  6. Ready to serve with any sauce.
